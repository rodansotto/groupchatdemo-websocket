Group Chat - A WebSocket Demo
* Press Connect button to start chatting. This will connect to wss://socketsbay.com/wss/v2/2/demo/, the free Websocket server from SocketsBay.
* Press Disconnect button when done.
* Press New Chat Window button to open a new chat window. You can open as many windows as you want. They will all send messages to each other.
* You can also open this chat on another device with a browser, even on your smartphone with a browser.
